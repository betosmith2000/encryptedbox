Features

Decentralized

Decentralized Apps are not controlled by any single authority. Users own their identities and data. And most importantly, Encrypted Box does not save user data.

Blockstack ID

Blockstack ID provides user-controlled login and storage that enable you to take back control of your identity and data. It's blockchain-based security and encryption.

Storage

Encrypted Box allows you to store your passwords and notes securely.

Share your secrets

If you need to share a note or password, you only need to have the recipient's Blockstack ID and Encrypted Box will generate a file that no one but the recipient can read.

Encrypted

All information stored in the Encrypted Box is encrypted. When you share a file, the information is decrypted and a new file is generated that is encrypted with the recipient's public key so that only the recipient can access its content.

Ad free

Access to Encrypted Box will remain free and without ads so you can enjoy a clean and intuitive interface. You can support us by following in our social networks, with likes and telling us that we can improve!
